🗓 Booking & Payment

The easiest way is to message us directly on WhatsApp (+855 96 766 4626) — we usually reply within a few hours. You can also fill in the enquiry form on our Contact page or email us at kungkeatravelandtours@gmail.com.

Tell us your preferred tour, arrival date, number of guests, and where you're staying in Stung Treng and we'll confirm availability immediately.

For half-day and full-day tours, 1–2 days' notice is usually enough — sometimes even same-day if you message us early in the morning. For the 3-night Ultimate Nature & Food Tour, we recommend booking at least 3–5 days ahead so we can arrange accommodation and all the logistics.

During peak season (November to February) we do get busy, so the earlier you contact us the better.

For half-day and full-day tours, no deposit is required. We confirm your booking by message and you pay on the day of the tour.

For the 3-night tour, we ask for a small deposit to cover accommodation pre-booking. We'll discuss the details with you when you enquire.

We accept cash in US Dollars or Cambodian Riel on the day of your tour. USD is preferred and widely used throughout Cambodia. We can also accept payment via ABA bank transfer if arranged in advance — just ask us when you book.

We understand that travel plans change. For day tours, please let us know at least 24 hours before your tour start time and we'll happily reschedule or cancel with no charge.

For the 3-night tour, cancellations with less than 48 hours notice may incur a small fee to cover accommodation that has already been booked. We'll always try to find a fair solution.

If you have a full day, the Waterfalls & Mekong Boat Cruises tour gives you the complete picture — river, jungle, and a swim beneath the falls. Most guests say it was their favourite day in Cambodia.

🎒 What to Bring & Wear

We recommend bringing:

  • ☀️ Sunscreen and a hat — the Mekong sun is strong, especially on the boat
  • 🦟 Insect repellent — particularly useful in the flooded forest
  • 👕 Light, breathable clothing — long sleeves are good for sun and mosquito protection
  • 👟 Comfortable closed-toe shoes or sandals with straps
  • 📷 Camera or phone for photos
  • 💊 Any personal medication you may need

We provide drinking water throughout. For full-day tours, meals are included so you don't need to bring food.

Light, comfortable clothes that you don't mind getting slightly wet. A long-sleeved shirt provides good protection from both the sun and insects. We provide life jackets on the boat — these are mandatory for all guests regardless of swimming ability.

For the kayaking tour, wear clothes you're happy to get wet in, and bring a dry change of clothes in a bag.

Please avoid bringing single-use plastic bottles — we provide refillable water on all tours. Valuables like laptops or large amounts of cash are best left at your guesthouse. The boats have storage space but they're not safe for electronics in rough weather.

🗺️ Getting to Stung Treng

Stung Treng is accessible by bus or minivan from several major Cambodian cities:

  • 🚌 From Phnom Penh: ~7–8 hours by bus or minivan (Giant Ibis and Mekong Express are reliable operators)
  • 🚌 From Siem Reap: ~5–6 hours by minivan
  • 🚌 From Kratie: ~2–3 hours by minivan — Kratie is a great stop to combine with Stung Treng
  • 🛺 From the Lao border (Dom Kralor): ~30–45 minutes by tuk-tuk or taxi

If you need help arranging transport to Stung Treng, just ask us — we can point you to the best options.

Yes — Stung Treng is a very safe, quiet provincial town. Crime targeting tourists is extremely rare. The biggest practical consideration is the heat and sun on the river, which is why we recommend sun protection and early morning departures. Our guides are with you throughout every tour.

There are several good guesthouses and small hotels in Stung Treng town, most within easy walking distance of the river. We're happy to recommend places to stay that suit your budget — just ask when you message us. We collect all guests directly from their accommodation so there's no need to arrange your own transport to the meeting point.

November to February is the dry season and the most comfortable time to visit — cooler temperatures, clear skies, and calm river conditions. This is peak season.

June to October is the wet season. The river rises dramatically and the flooded forests become even more extraordinary — the water level can be 8–10 metres higher than the dry season, turning the entire landscape into an otherworldly submerged world. Tours still run in the wet season and many guests prefer it for the dramatic scenery, though some waterfall trails may be inaccessible.

March to May is the hot season — very warm but still a good time to visit. The waterfalls are at their most impressive just after the rains begin in May.

🍚 Food, Health & Special Needs

Yes — please let us know your dietary requirements when you book. Traditional Khmer food uses a lot of fresh vegetables, rice, and river fish. Vegetarian meals are easy to arrange. Vegan is also possible with a little notice. If you have severe allergies, please tell us in advance so we can take care of everything.

We provide sealed bottled drinking water on all tours. Please do not drink directly from the river. In Stung Treng town, stick to bottled or filtered water as you would anywhere in Cambodia.

We're a tour company, not medical advisors — please consult your doctor or a travel health clinic before visiting Cambodia. That said, most travellers to Stung Treng take standard precautions: hepatitis A and typhoid vaccinations are commonly recommended for rural Cambodia. Malaria risk in this region is low but some travellers choose prophylactics — your doctor can advise.

Mosquito repellent is strongly recommended, particularly in the flooded forest.

The boat cruise tours involve stepping onto and off a longtail boat from a riverbank, which requires some balance and mobility. The waterfalls tour includes walking on uneven forest trails. If you have specific mobility needs, please message us before booking and we'll be honest about what's feasible and suggest the best options for you.
