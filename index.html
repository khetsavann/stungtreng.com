Welcome to Stung Treng

Nestled where the Mekong and Sekong rivers meet, Stung Treng is one of Cambodia's last untouched frontiers. We are a local, family-run tour company offering authentic river and jungle experiences you won't find anywhere else.

As your local guide, the Kungkea team brings you face-to-face with the massive thundering waterfalls, the mighty Mekong River, beautiful river islands & flooded forests, local fishing villages, and jungle wildlife — all with the warmth of genuine Khmer hospitality.
