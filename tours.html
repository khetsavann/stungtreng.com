Half Day

Mekong Boat Cruise Through Flooded Forests

⏱ 4–5 Hours 👥 Small Group 🌅 Morning Departure

Glide through the extraordinary flooded forests of the Mekong River on a private longtail boat. This is one of Southeast Asia's most unique river ecosystems — a labyrinth of submerged trees, mirror-still waterways, and abundant birdlife that few travellers ever witness.

Your local guide will navigate through hidden channels between remote river islands, stopping to spot wildlife, visit a local fishing family, and experience the stillness of one of Cambodia's last wild river frontiers.

  • ✅ English-speaking local guide
  • ✅ Hotel pickup & drop-off in Stung Treng town
  • ✅ Drinking water
  • ✅ Life jackets

Half Day

Kayaking & Boat Cruise Through Mekong Flooded Forests

⏱ 4–5 Hours 👥 Small Group 🌅 Morning Departure

Combine the thrill of kayaking with a relaxing boat cruise through the Mekong's flooded forest. Paddle your own kayak through narrow jungle waterways inaccessible by motor boat — then switch to the longtail for wider stretches and island hopping.

This tour is perfect for active travellers who want both adventure and serenity. No prior kayaking experience needed — the channels are calm and beginner-friendly.

Full Day

Waterfalls & Mekong Boat Cruises

⏱ Full Day 👥 Small Group 🍚 Lunch Included

The best of Stung Treng in a single day. Start with a morning boat cruise through the Mekong's flooded forests, then venture out to the province's thundering waterfalls — a hidden gem unknown to most Cambodia travellers.

Swim beneath the falls, enjoy a fresh Khmer lunch prepared by a local family, and return to town by late afternoon. This is the tour most guests say was their favourite day in Cambodia.

The Ultimate Nature & Food Tour

⏱ 3 Nights / 4 Days 👥 Small Group 🍚 All Meals Included
